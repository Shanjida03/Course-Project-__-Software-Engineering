House Rental System
A web-based house rental platform that connects renters and landlords, making it easy to find, book, and pay for houses, flats, and rooms online.

📌 Project Overview
The House Rental System is designed to replace the manual process of finding and renting houses with a simple, fast, and fully digital solution. Users can search for available properties by area, room type, and budget — without physically visiting the location.

🎯 Goals

Allow customers to register, search, and book houses online
Connect renters directly with landlords through a single platform
Support open-source deployment on Android smart phones
Save time and effort for both renters and property owners
